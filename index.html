DicePass - Random Passpharse Generator

DicePass is based on EFF's Dice which allows one to generate a reasonably secure random pass phrase that is easier to remember than random characters.

TLDR; click the button and get a locally produced cryptographically sound random passpharse you can use as a password and (more easily) remember

Long version: The original idea is based upon throwing a dice (or set of dices) to generate a 5 digits number. You then look that number in a large list of words where each word is associated with one number from 11111 to 66666. If you do that for 6 times you get a rather long
